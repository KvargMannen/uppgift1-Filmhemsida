Bladerunner 2049

A 2017 American neo-noir science fiction film directed by Denis Villeneuve and is a sequel to the 1982 movie Blade Runner. In the year 2049, 30 years following the events of Blade Runner, bioengineered humans known as replicants are slaves. Officer K, a replicant, works for the Los Angeles Police Department as a "blade runner", an officer who hunts and kills rogue replicants. On his latest mission after killing a replicant on a remote farm he discovers a long lost secret that could set society spiraling in to chaos.
